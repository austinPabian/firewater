    Two ways to do it

    The Firewater Way

    Catalog

    Our signature method.

    Our trademark style of service, the Firewater Catalog is ideal for teams or larger groups looking to order varying quantities of differing designs. This order style starts with a digital showcase consisting of apparel that we curate with you for your team to order from. The catalog is coupled with a live order form online, making it easy for you to share it with your team and have everyone mark down what they want before we lock it and get it ordered up!

    Standard

    The typical method.

    This is the route that suits most standard apparel orders. Need one design put on some tees and a couple hoodies for the gang? Can do. Maybe some embroidered polos for the office? Sure thing. For any and all custom apparel, be it 12 or 1,200 shirts, we'll work with you to make it happen. Simply put: you tell us what you need, we send you a proof, and once you're happy with it, we get it rolling with the print facility. Easy-peasy.
